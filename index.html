Welcome to HDFCRAFT GitHub Pages.

Minecraft is a popular game among K-12 students. Minecraft allows players to manipuate textured cubes in a 3D generated world. This poster presents how to use NASA Earth science data in HDF to create a Minecraft world. The goal is to give students an early exposure to NASA Earth data and let them explore a world observed by NASA satellites with Minecraft.
